Slow Cooker Butter Chicken

  • Time: 4 h 30 m
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 2 tbs
Vegetable Oil 2 tbs
Skinless, boneless chicken thighs 4 thighs
Onion 1 onion
Garlic 3 cloves
Curry Powder 2 tsp
Curry Paste 1 tbs
Tandoori Masala 2 tsp
Garam Masala 1 tsp
Tomato Paste 1 can (6 oz)
Green Cardamom Pods 15 pods
Low Fat Plain Yogurt 1 cup
Coconut Milk 1 can (14 oz)

Step 1: Melt the butter and vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in the chicken, onion, and garlic. Cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 10 minutes. Stir in the curry powder, curry paste, tandoori masala, garam masala, and tomato paste until no lumps of tomato paste remain. Pour into a slow cooker, and stir in the cardamom pods, coconut milk, and yogurt. Season to taste with salt.

Step 2: Cook on High 4 to 6 hours, or on Low 6 to 8 hours until the chicken is tender and the sauce has reduced to your desired consistency. Remove and discard the cardamom pods before serving.

Source: allrecipes.com

Okroshka

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: https://www.russianfood.com/recipes/recipe.php?rid=132831
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 4
Eggs 4
Cucumbers 2
Radish 8
Boiled sausage 300g
Kvass or kefir, or ayran 1L

Step 1: Potatoes (peeled) and eggs need to be boiled (eggs immediately in cold water).

Step 2: Cucumbers, radishes, onions and greens are finely chopped, sausage is also put into cubes.

Step 3: Cut the cooled potatoes and eggs in the same way.

Step 4: Pour all this into a saucepan and mix well. It is advisable, of course, to fill the pan to the very top. I do not pour the okroshka immediately - it is stored longer, and in general, it is more convenient.

Step 5: So. I wanted a portion of the tastiest okroshechki. To do this, I pour our billet into a deep bowl and pour it with kvass / kefir / ayran - to choose from. I add a teaspoon of mustard to the bowl without a slide.
